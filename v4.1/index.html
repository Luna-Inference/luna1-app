Step 1 of 3

Plug in Your Luna Device

You'll need this black power adapter:

Here's exactly what to do:

  1. Find the power adapter - It's the black rectangular box with a cable
  2. Plug the small end into Luna - Look for the round hole on the side of your Luna device
  3. Plug the other end into the wall - Use any regular wall outlet in your home
  4. That's it! - You should see a small light turn on
Step 2 of 3

Connect Luna to Your Computer

You'll need this cable (it looks like a thick phone charger):

Now let's connect Luna to your computer:

  1. Find the network cable - It's the thick cable with rectangular plugs on both ends
  2. Plug one end into Luna - Look for the rectangular hole on the back/side of Luna (next to where you plugged in power)
  3. Plug the other end into your computer - Look for a similar rectangular hole on your laptop or computer
  4. Push firmly! - You should hear a little "click" when it's properly connected

Note: If your computer doesn't have this type of port, you may need to use a USB adapter (check your Luna box for one).
